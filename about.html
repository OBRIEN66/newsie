The Newsie Club

What is Newsie Club?

Newsie Club is a email newsletter client for the modern age. In a day when we are always charging around, sometimes it's good to slow down and read. We here at Newsie Club still cling to the roots of the internet, the ideas it was founded on: people sharing knowledge. Email newsletters are a long-standing tradition to pass interesting tidbits around the internet to equally interesting people. It's not a tradition we want to see die because people don't have enough time.
